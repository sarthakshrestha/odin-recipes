Momo Recipe

Steamed dumpling with some form of filling, mostly chicken. It is a delicacy in Tibet, Nepal, Bhutan and the Indian communities of Darjeeling, Sikkim and Kalimpong.

    Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Ground chicken thighs
  • Chopped Onions
  • Minced Garlics
  • Minced peeled garlics
  • Group cumin
  • Salt & Black pepper
  • Nonstick cooking spray
